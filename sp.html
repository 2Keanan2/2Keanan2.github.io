Identity, Values and Stengths.

  • Q: Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on the decision now.
  • A: I'm not sure I have made an ethical decision in the past. I have made decisions that I thought were right at the time, but I'm not sure if they were ethical.
    • Q: Describe how your culture and the people around you have influenced your values and identity.
  • A: My culture has influenced my values and identity in many ways. For example, my family has always valued honesty and virtue, and I have tried to live by those values.
  • Q: Identify your strengths and how they will support you during your learning journey.
  • A: My strengths include my ability to learn quickly, my creativity, and my ability to work well in any area I put my mind to.
  • Q: Evaluate your limitations in terms of your learning and career development. How might these affect your learning journey?
  • A: My limitations include my lack of experience in certain areas, my tendency to procrastinate, and my lack of confidence.
  • Q: Share an example of when you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss strategies you tried at the time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now and why.
  • A: I have had many experiences working with others, but one that stands out was when I was working in the fast food industry and I had a way of doing something that was different from my colleagues. They were resistant to my way of doing things, and there was tension between us that ended up ruining the business.
