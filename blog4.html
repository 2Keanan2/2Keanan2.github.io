Blog: Foundations Reflection

I'm feeling a bit uncertain about what to write for the Sprint 5 blog titled 'Foundations Reflection' because there isn't a clear set of questions to guide my thoughts. When I started the foundations program, I expected it to be more structured and led by teachers, perhaps similar to a traditional textbook approach. However, it turned out to be quite different and more challenging than I anticipated, which caught me off guard.

Despite these challenges, I believe I did relatively well, especially considering my limited background in coding. I managed to adapt and learn throughout the process, which gives me confidence as I continue with the course. Even if I don't end up working in this field, I feel that the skills and knowledge I'm gaining will still be valuable and beneficial for my future endeavors.
